A pipeline wakes a sequence of cold-tier services on a schedule. Use it when a tool's internal cron (Airflow DAGs, Airbyte connection schedules, dbt cosmos schedules) needs the service to be UP at trigger time. The pipeline does not run jobs, it just turns the lights on so the tools can fire their own work.
